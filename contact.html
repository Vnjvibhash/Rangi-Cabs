Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a taxi?

You can book a taxi through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service number. We recommend booking in advance for better availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and cash payments. For online bookings, payment can be made through our secure payment gateway.

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we provide airport pickup and drop services. Our drivers track your flight status and ensure timely service. Waiting time is included in the package.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours before the scheduled time are free of charge. For cancellations within 24 hours, a nominal fee may be charged depending on the service type.
